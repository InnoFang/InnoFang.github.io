About me

Hi, I am Inno, an undergraduate student in Computer Science from North University of China (Taiyuan, China).I am passionate about open source, love sharing and communication. I am a programming enthusiast, aspire to be a fun and creative developer. My mainly skill now is software development. At college, I was the former head of NUC Android Lab (2017 - 2018). However, I am learning machine learning now and I have a strong interest. In addition, I am interested in data analysis, data visualization, creative programming, recommendation system, etc. Well, my interests are very wide, but the interest is the best teacher, isn’t it? Although I am still a beginner, I hope to be a graduate student in this field, and then continue to study in depth. If you want to have more communication with me, check out my Github profile, or send me an e-mail. I am looking forward to talking with you.
